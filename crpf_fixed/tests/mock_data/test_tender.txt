ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

1. FINANCIAL CRITERIA
The bidder shall have minimum annual turnover of Rs. 5,00,00,000
(Rupees Five Crore only) as per audited accounts for the last
financial year. Proof: CA-certified Balance Sheet or IT Returns.

2. TECHNICAL EXPERIENCE
The bidder must have successfully completed at least 3 (three)
similar works of construction of buildings/civil works of value
not less than Rs. 1 Crore each during the last 5 (five) years.
Proof: Work Completion Certificates from government clients.

3. COMPLIANCE
The bidder shall possess a valid GST Registration Certificate.
Proof: GST Registration Certificate.

4. CERTIFICATION
The bidder shall hold a valid ISO 9001:2015 certification.
Proof: Valid ISO certificate with expiry date not lapsed.
